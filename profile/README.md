# HRP Lab

HRP Lab is a public research and cloud-lab organisation for Trident-G theory, methods, ethics, and platform experiments.

## What this organization is for

This organization hosts open work around Trident-G adaptive-intelligence theory, research-platform scaffolding, ground-truth documentation, and public methods for responsible experimentation.

The lab is used to separate public research material from private operational systems, while keeping the public record readable and reusable.

## Public repositories

- `research-platform` - Cloud Lab and Basement Lab research-platform work.
- `Cloud-Lab-Ground-Truth` - component maps and protocol boundaries for Trident Cloud Lab.
- `Trident-G-theory` - public theory material for Trident-G adaptive general intelligence.
- `trident-g-open-methods-and-ethics` - public methods and ethics documentation.

## Principles

HRP Lab work should be transparent where possible, explicit about boundaries, careful with evidence, and clear about which material is experimental, theoretical, operational, or ready for reuse.
